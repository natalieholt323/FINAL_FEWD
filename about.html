Brooklyn! It is full of bodegas. And in those bodegas, There are cats.

Bodega cats.

This site is a digital CATalog (yup) of bodega cats in New York City. The site visualizes public pics of cats posted to Bodega Cats of Instagram on a city map, locating them by the latitude and longitude coordinates embedded in their metadata. The cats were accessed via publicly available APIs provided by popular photo sharing websites.

To add a personal touch, I've also included illustrations and photos of some of my favorite bodega cats in Brooklyn. I like to go visit these guys whenever I'm in their 'hood, but I can't always keep track of where they all live. Now I know where to find them, and you do, too!
