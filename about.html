Brooklyn! It is full of bodegas. And in those bodegas, There are cats.

Bodega cats.

This site is a digital CATalog (yup) of my favorite bodega cats in New York City. I like to go visit these guys whenever I'm in their 'hood, but I can't always keep track of where they all live. Now I know where to find them, and you do, too! Go ahead, browse the map.

The idea was partly inspired by my own urban escapades, partly inspired by Bodega Cats of Instagram . I wanted to find a way to map/visualize my casual knowledge of these kitties. The result, I hope, is a useful resource for fellow bodega cat aficionados - and maybe it's entertaining for fans of personalized urban knowledge.
